Why this topic?

We chose to conduct research about this topic as it has a significant impact on how people live their lives, affecting their access to opportunities, standard of education, access to healthcare, and general well-being. A more inclusive and prosperous future can be fostered by raising public understanding of the effects of socioeconomic status, which can result in more equal societies and improved lives for all citizens.
